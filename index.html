Who am I?

My name is Denzell Devonte Dixon, I am a programmer, an immigrant, an astronomer and most of all an engineer. I love computers, animation, programming, helping others and building things. I want to be apart of the future. I want to help build quantum computers, develop genetic algorithims and create the world's first artficial inteligence. But for now, I will settle with making my own personal website.

Who do I want to be?

In college, I would like to study both Computer Science and Computer Engineering. I would like to become an engineer that has a strong foundation on both the cpu and the code. I believe as technology ventures into a new type of computing, we will need to go back to the drawing board. We will need to go back to the transister so we can develop a new form of computing and a new future for technolgy.

Partner in Crime

When programming, there is a limit to how much that you can do by yourself. I have learned this the hard way. As a result of this, I have made many friends in the programming field. Someone who I occasionaly work together on programming projects with is my friend Omar Cobas.
